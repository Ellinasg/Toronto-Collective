Collective: the new collaboration of HEISTDRO & NINJAH, the grown hobby shop of Toronto. Whatever the creative whim, Collective can provide the best quality tools to satisfy. Above all else, Collective is a welcoming space for innovative, underground minds. Your niche hobbies are at home here. As the Canadian supplier of Krink, we’re stocked with graffiti supplies, also from Montana Black and Canton. Vintage games and videos, comics from Marvel, DC and Image, original prints and art work, local clothing including MARTIAL ARTISTIK, and an international collection of designer toys. Collective welcomes local artists and enthusiasts of all subcultures - our space is always free for a cypher, quick game, sketch or viewing. Whatever your thing, Collective is home for you and your creativity - come chill and see for yourself!
